TrackerExtension

Lightweight Chrome extension that tracks sent and received messages on Gmail and LinkedIn. Data stays on your device. Optionally, it can email you a daily CSV report using your Gmail account with your explicit consent.

What it does

  • Counts messages you send and receive on Gmail and LinkedIn.
  • Stores counts and deduplication keys in chrome.storage.local only.
  • No servers; nothing is transmitted to us.
  • Optional: send yourself a daily CSV summary via Gmail API (scope: gmail.send).

Privacy-first

We do not collect or sell personal data. Message content is not uploaded; minimal metadata is used locally for counting and deduplication.
